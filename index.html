Botanical Cells (Greywater)

Four 300L indoor planter cells contain banana plants, papyrus, canna lilies, and ornamental grasses. These plants absorb nutrients and filter water through root systems. Gravel layers provide mechanical filtration. After 5-7 days, water is clean enough for outdoor gardens.

Septic System (Blackwater)

Conventional septic tank (1,500L capacity) with anaerobic digestion breaks down solid waste. Effluent flows to outdoor leach field (200 sq ft) with perforated pipes in gravel trenches. Soil bacteria complete treatment before water reaches groundwater.

Composting Toilet Option

Alternative: dry composting toilet eliminates blackwater entirely. Separates solids (composted with sawdust for 12-18 months) from liquids (diluted for landscaping). Reduces water use by 75L daily and produces nutrient-rich compost for non-edible plants.
